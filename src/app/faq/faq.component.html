Frequently Asked Questions

From the Home screen, click the bright "Sign In" button in the navigation bar.
Click on the "Register Here" link at the bottom of the Sign In page.
Enter your email address and set a password. Then, proceed onto the next screen.
Enter your name, and any additional details you would like. Then, proceed to the next screen.
Set your security questions, then click the "Create Account" button. Welcome!

The ability to reset your password is only available if you already have an account with Bob's Computer Repair Shop. If you do not currently have an account, be sure to Create an Account instead. From the Home screen, click the bright "Sign In" button in the navigation bar.

Click on the "Reset Password" link at the bottom of the Sign In page.
Enter your email address. Then, proceed onto the next screen.
Answer your security questions. Then, proceed onto the next screen.
Finally, enter your password and click "Change Password". Well done! Now you can sign in with your new user credentials.

The ability to configure employees is only available if you are signed in as an Administrator. If you do not currently have an Administrator account, be sure to connect with your administrator to request higher access privileges. From the Home screen, click the "Employee Configuration" link in the navigation bar.

This is the Employee Configuration portal. To Add a New Employee, click the "Add an Employee" button above the list of employees.
Enter the new employee's information. Then, click the "Submit" button to return to the Configuration portal and view your updated list of employees.
On the Employee Configuration Portal, you also have the ability to edit existing employee records. Click on the Pen icon to the right side of a listed employee to edit their information.
Change whichever employee data needs to be updated. Then, click "Submit" to return to the Portal and view the updated records. If you have no changes to make, click the "Return to User List" link at the bottom of the page.
You also have the ability to deactivate an employee record. To do this, click on the Trash Can icon next to an employee.
You will see a pop-up message that asks for confirmation. Click "OK" to deactivate that employee's account. Click "Cancel" to change your mind and close the pop-up.
Deactivated employees will have a red "Inactive" status on their record.
